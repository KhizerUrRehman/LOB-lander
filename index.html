League of Basketball (LOB)

Bringing Professional Basketball to Pakistan

League of Basketball (LOB) is an organization formed by the collective efforts of basketball players all over Karachi. Our mission is to establish a professional standard league for the first time in Pakistan. In addition to our flagship league, we also run WLOB for women and LOB Varsity for younger students. LOB is a non-profit organization, and our sole purpose is to enhance the basketball scene in Pakistan.
