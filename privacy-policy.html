Privacy Policy

for the League of Basketball (LOB) mobile application

Last Updated: August 18, 2025

The League of Basketball ("LOB," "we," "our," "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have when using the LOB mobile application (the “App”).

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you use the App:

  • Personal Information (if you provide it): name, email address, phone number, profile details, and registration information for leagues or events.
  • Usage Data: in-app actions, pages viewed, and performance metrics to help us improve stability and features.
  • Device Information: device model, operating system, app version, IP address, and mobile identifiers.
  • Location Data (if enabled): approximate or precise location to support features like nearby courts or events.
  • Notifications: push notification tokens to deliver updates about games, schedules, and announcements.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the App and its features.
  • Register and manage players, teams, and event participation.
  • Send service-related messages, announcements, and reminders.
  • Monitor and enhance performance, security, and user experience.
  • Prevent fraud, misuse, or violations of our policies.
  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations.

3. Legal Basis (Where Applicable)

Where required (e.g., in the EEA/UK), we rely on one or more legal bases: your consent, performance of a contract, compliance with a legal obligation, or our legitimate interests (such as improving and securing the App).

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share data:

  • With your consent.
  • With trusted service providers who process data on our behalf (e.g., hosting, analytics, crash reporting) under confidentiality obligations.
  • To comply with legal requests, enforce policies, or protect rights, safety, and property.
  • In connection with organizational changes (e.g., merger or restructuring), consistent with this Policy.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect your information. No security measures are perfect, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that a child under 13 has provided personal data, we will delete it promptly.

8. Your Rights & Choices

  • Access, update, or delete certain personal information within the App or by contacting us.
  • Opt out of non-essential communications.
  • Where applicable, request restriction or object to certain processing.
  • Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent.

Your rights may vary depending on your location and applicable law.

9. International Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own. We take steps to ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, consistent with applicable laws.

10. Third-Party Services & Links

The App may link to third-party websites or integrate services such as analytics and crash reporting. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies.

11. Cookies & Similar Technologies

The App itself does not use traditional browser cookies. Mobile SDKs and similar technologies may be used for analytics, crash reporting, or notifications. You can manage permissions in your device settings.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Last Updated” date above and, where appropriate, provide additional notice.
